En Fraccionamiento Rancho Nuevo nos hemos comprometido a crear espacios residenciales únicos, donde la calidad, el diseño y la integración con la naturaleza se convierten en la esencia de cada hogar.

Nuestro profundo conocimiento de la región y de sus ventajas nos permite ofrecer un estilo de vida excepcional. Cada detalle ha sido cuidadosamente pensado para aprovechar la ubicación privilegiada, facilitando el acceso a servicios de primer nivel y creando un ambiente que une modernidad y tradición.

Fieles a nuestros valores, en Fraccionamiento Rancho Nuevo nos comprometemos a seguir desarrollando casas contemporáneas en perfecta armonía con su entorno natural, con especial énfasis en la seguridad. Contamos con iluminación estratégica en todas las áreas, caseta de vigilancia y portón automático, lo que garantiza un entorno protegido y sereno para que las familias puedan construir recuerdos inolvidables y disfrutar de una experiencia residencial plena y segura.

Inspired by a Nordic lifestyle in which harmony and smart design are key, the new residential project LE LAGOM offers an oasis of comfort in the very heart of nature.

Living LAGOM?

A Swedish lifestyle approach of “right” choices, balance, ethics and respect for the environment.

Something “lagom” is optimal, both in terms of quality and quantity. Living lagom means appreciating things as they are, without necessarily wanting more.

Single-family houses, built entirely of wooden beams and posts.

A variety of homes close to and in perfect harmony with nature will be available. This makes it easier to choose the design and build that really suit you. In these solid wood frame homes, even the interior finish is of wood. The walls and ceilings feature a noble material that adds a warming touch. Blending modern and rustic, each home captures ample natural light and exudes freedom. When simplicity of design reigns, happiness and harmony follow.

Whatever model you choose, its elegant design will satisfy your most demanding requirements. These homes impress everyone with the creativity of their design, in particular the spectacular windows. And nature not only surrounds you, it inspires the interiors, making the homes as beautiful inside as out.

A life choice for a life of choices

Buyers will truly appreciate the layout of the properties. Facing south or west, the residences respect your desire for peace and quiet in the heart of nature. Some border a protected area, while others are on Le Géant golf course. All are in the heart of a deciduous forest, adding to the sought-after privacy.

Since the summit is just minutes away, active and sporty families will feel thoroughly fulfilled. The resort offers a multitude of activities, businesses, services and places of entertainment.
